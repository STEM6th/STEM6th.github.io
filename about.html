About Us

Who are we?

AblifyNow of Egypt is a statewide nonprofit organization here to help create a society where disabled figures are heard, appreciated, and loved. AblifyNow of Egypt is here to help create a society where disabled figures are heard, appreciated, and loved. AblifyNow of Egypt has educational seminars, fundraisers, donation drives, and volunteering events that help show their love for the disabled community! Go check out what upcoming events that are happening!

What are our goals?

AblifyNow of Egypt's end goal is to help educate people around the world about the hardships that people in disabled communities face as well as help disabled communities feel more accepted and be able to make life easier for disabled communities around the world by raising enough money so that they can get the support that they need. This goal is only achievable if we worked together, really understood thier problems and pure will to help them.
